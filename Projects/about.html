About Us

Our Mission

The mission of Gyanodaya Vidya Mandir is to seek exceptionally promising students from all backgrounds across the society and to educate them, through mental discipline and social experience, to develop their intellectual, moral, civic, and creative capacities to the fullest. The larger aim of this institute is to prepare and stimulate each new generation to perpetuate and advance human knowledge and to contribute to the health and development of the human community
